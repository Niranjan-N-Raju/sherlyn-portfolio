Hi! I'm Sherilyn!

“I'm a UI/UX Designer based in Bangalore, India, with 8 years of creative adventures under my belt. Originally diving into the world of Fashion Merchandising, I've morphed into a multi-hat-wearing enthusiast - I'm a product designer, writer, entrepreneur, and content creator.

I specialize in crafting delightful experiences for SaaS products, THINK- jazzy dashboards, and nifty mobile apps.

Born in bustling Mumbai and now soaking up Bangalore's vibes, I've got a soft spot for all things tech, especially AI, Chatbots, and NLP. Love cooking up designs that weave these futuristic marvels together!

By day, I'm a Senior UX Engineer at H&R Block Australia. Off the clock, you'll find me behind a lens capturing moments, getting crafty, and travelling.

Drop a line, let's chat - I'm all about connecting and making cool stuff happen!"
